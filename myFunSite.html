Paradiso

June 23-24

Since gracing the Pacific Northwest with its presence in 2012, Paradiso of USC Events has continued to deliver us a quality experience. This year, the lineup comes to us earlier than ever, giving you plenty of time to plan for the biggest Paradiso yet! With past lineups headlining performances from Skrillex, Hardwell, and Bassnectar, this year gives us plenty to look forward to as well, featuring Porter Robinson, Marshmello, Tiesto, Zeds Dead, Seven Lions, Zomboy, and Snails among many other exciting Northwest favorites!

EDC Las Vegas

June 16-18

The hottest music festival in the United States is clearly EDC Las Vegas. Not only is it in the middle of the Nevada desert, it's got headline acts you can't see anywhere else. Case in point: This year's festival is topped by a world exclusive Alison Wonderland B2B Diplo B2B Jauz set. So, you know, bring ear plugs to protect your drums from all that bass.

Fvded In the Park

July 7-8

FVDED In The Park is back in 2017 for its third year at Holland Park in Surrey, on July 7 and 8. This year Blueprint and Live Nation Canada have signed on The Chainsmokers, Wiz Khalifa, PartyNextDoor, Dillon Francis, Ty Dolla $ign and many more artists over three stages during the two-day high energy festival.

Pemberton

July 13-16

Chance the Rapper, Muse, A Tribe Called Quest, Major Lazer and many more artists are among the acts announced on Monday (May 1) for the 2017 Pemberton Music Festival. The July 13-6 event in Pemberton, British Columbia, will also feature sets from Haim, Run the Jewels, Marshmello, Diplo, Big Sean and others.
